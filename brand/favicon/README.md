## Favicon & Touch Icons

Provide the diamond symbol (no text) on transparent background where possible.

Required files:
- `favicon-16.png`
- `favicon-32.png`
- `apple-touch-icon.png` (180Ã—180 or larger)

Guidance:
- Keep the mark centered with generous padding.
- Use `diamond-mark--light` on dark backgrounds and `diamond-mark--dark` on light backgrounds. For favicon PNG, prefer transparent background with dark mark for most sites.
- Flat color only; no effects.
