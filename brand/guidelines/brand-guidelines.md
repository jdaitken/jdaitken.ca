# JD Media Brand Guidelines (Diamond Mark)

## Brand Overview
- **Tone:** Modern, confident, premium, minimal.
- **Principles:** Clarity over decoration, geometric precision, consistent spacing, no unnecessary effects.
- **Usage:** This kit centers on the geometric diamond symbol (no text).

## Logo Usage
- **Primary mark:** Diamond symbol only.
- **Versions:** `diamond-mark--light` (white for dark backgrounds), `diamond-mark--dark` (black for light backgrounds), `diamond-mark--mono` (single-color utility).
- **Clear space:** At least 0.5× the mark’s width on all sides.
- **Minimum size:** 24px on screen; 8mm in print.
- **Backgrounds:** Use `--light` on dark/colored backgrounds; `--dark` on light backgrounds; `--mono` on single-color or constrained contexts.

## Do / Don’t
- **Do:** Keep proportions, maintain clear space, use provided colors, keep it flat (no gradients/shadows/glow).
- **Don’t:** Stretch, rotate, add outlines, add effects, place on busy photos without contrast, recolor outside the palette, add text inside the mark.

## Color Palette (HEX)
- **Background / Charcoal:** `#0B1220`
- **Surface:** `#131B2B`
- **Text / Soft white:** `#E9EEF5`
- **Muted:** `#98A1B3`
- **Border:** `#24304A`
- **Accent (Gold, optional):** `#C6A24A`

## Typography
- **Primary:** DM Sans (weights: 400, 500, 700). Sizes: base 16px; headings scale up from tokens.
- **Fallback:** Inter, then system sans.
- **Usage:** Tight letter-spacing for headings (-0.02em). Line-height 1.2–1.5.

## Accessibility
- Ensure text/background contrast meets WCAG AA (use soft white on charcoal, muted text only on surface backgrounds).
- Preserve focus states on interactive elements.
- Avoid placing the mark on low-contrast or patterned backgrounds without a solid overlay.

## Export Checklist
- **Web:** SVG + PNG (1x, 2x) for light/dark/mono; name per convention.
- **Favicon:** `favicon-16.png`, `favicon-32.png`, `apple-touch-icon.png`.
- **Social:** Square SVG/PNG/WebP at 512×512 or larger with safe padding.
- **Watermark:** Use `diamond-mark--mono` as a small (12–24px) overlay with 30–60% opacity on contrasting areas.
